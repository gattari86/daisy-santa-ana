Frequently Asked Questions

How much does treatment cost? +

Treatment costs vary depending on your skin condition and personalized plan. Your first consultation is completely free, and we'll give you a clear breakdown of costs before you commit to anything. We offer affordable treatment plans designed to fit your budget.

How many sessions will I need? +

Most patients see visible improvement within 2 weeks. The total number of sessions depends on your skin's condition. During your free consultation, we'll outline an estimated treatment timeline so you know what to expect.

Is the treatment painful? +

Our treatments are designed to be comfortable. You may feel mild sensations during extractions, but our experienced team works carefully to minimize any discomfort. Most patients describe the experience as relaxing.

Is it safe for sensitive skin? +

Yes. Every treatment plan is personalized to your skin type, including sensitive skin. We use no harsh oral medications and our topical treatments are tailored specifically for you. Your skin's safety is our priority.

Do you take insurance? +

We are a specialized skincare clinic and do not bill insurance directly. However, our treatment plans are competitively priced and we work with you to find a plan that fits your budget. Many patients find our care more affordable than co-pays for repeated dermatologist visits.

What products will I need to use at home? +

Your treatment plan may include recommended at-home products to support your results between visits. These are tailored to your specific skin needs and we'll explain exactly what each product does and why it's part of your plan.

What should I expect at my first visit? +

Your first visit is a free consultation where we assess your skin, discuss your concerns, and create a personalized treatment plan. There's no pressure and no obligation. You'll leave with a clear understanding of what we can do for your skin.

What if I can't make a Monday appointment? +

Our Santa Ana location is open on Mondays from 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM. If Mondays don't work for your schedule, we have multiple locations across Southern California with different availability. View all our locations and hours here.
